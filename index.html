Welcome to the Nefer Museum

Immerse yourself in the heart of ancient Egypt without leaving the city. The Nefer Museum offers an intimate encounter with a bygone era. New at Nefer

The Gold of the Pharaohs

Immerse yourself in the opulence of ancient Egypt. Discover the mastery of goldsmiths as you explore a dazzling collection of jewelry, masks, and ceremonial objects. Learn about the symbolism and significance of gold in Egyptian culture.

Gods and Goddesses of the Nile

Immerse yourself in the pantheon of Egyptian deities. Discover the stories and symbolism behind iconic figures like Isis, Osiris, and Horus. Marvel at the artistry and craftsmanship of these statues, once revered as intermediaries between humans and the divine. Unveil the secrets of ancient Egyptian religion through these magnificent representations of godly power.

Featured Artifact

Ram's-head Amulet

This amulet was probably made for a necklace worn by one of the Kushite kings. Representations show these pharaohs wearing a ram's-head amulet tied around the neck on a thick cord, the ends of which fall forward over the shoulders. Sometimes a smaller ram's head is attached to each end. Rams were associated with the god Amun, particularly in Nubia, where he was especially revered.
