Getting Here

The Nefer Museum is easily accessible by various modes of transportation:

  • Metro: Take Line 2 to the "Museum" station.
  • Bus: Several bus routes stop nearby. Visit the Alexandria Public Transportation website for detailed route information.
  • Taxi: Taxis are readily available throughout the city. Remember to agree on a fare with the driver before starting your journey.
  • Ride-Sharing: Utilize popular ride-sharing apps for a convenient transportation option.

Parking: Limited parking is available at the museum for a fee. We encourage our visitors to consider public transportation whenever possible.

Accessibility

The Nefer Museum is dedicated to providing a welcoming and accessible experience for all visitors. Our accessibility features include:

  • Wheelchair Accessibility: Ramps and elevators throughout the museum ensure ease of access for wheelchair users.
  • Accessible Restrooms: Designated restrooms, designed for visitors with mobility impairments, are available on each floor.
  • Assistive Listening Devices: For visitors with hearing impairments, we offer assistive listening devices. Please inquire at the admissions desk.
  • Large Print Materials: Large-print brochures and exhibit guides are available upon request.

Should you have any specific accessibility needs or require further assistance, please don't hesitate to contact us in advance. We are happy to help!
