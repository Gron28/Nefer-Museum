Accessibility

The Nefer Museum is dedicated to providing a welcoming and accessible environment for all visitors. We offer:

  • Wheelchair Accessibility: Ramps and elevators are available throughout the museum.
  • Accessible Restrooms: All restrooms are wheelchair accessible.
  • Assistive Listening Devices: Available upon request at the front desk.
  • Large-Print Materials: Large-print brochures and gallery guides are available.
  • Sensory-Friendly Hours: We offer sensory-friendly hours on the first Tuesday of each month from 9:00 AM to 10:00 AM. These hours feature reduced lighting and sound for a more relaxed experience.

If you have any specific accessibility needs, please do not hesitate to contact us in advance, and we will be happy to assist you in planning your visit.
